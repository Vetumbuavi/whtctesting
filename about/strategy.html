OUR STRATEGY

About

Welwitchia Health training Centre (University) “Where everyone is an achiever”, is a private institute for higher learning education in Namibia with a special focus on Health, Social and Management Science. It has placed teaching and learning, research and development, innovation and technology solutions, industry and community service, continuous professional development, and training and capacity development at the center of its operation. We take a unique and innovative approach to teaching that helps students connect with the subject matter they need to master.

Established: 2013

Vision

To be the Center of Excellence in Health, Social and Management Sciences in Namibia and beyond.

Mission

The Mission of WHTC is to develop and equip professionally qualified persons in the Health, Social and Management Sciences through Academic Pursuit and Practical On-Job Training.

Mandate

WHTC exists to provide Professional Education in Health, Social and Management Sciences to Namibian and International Students as provided in the University Charter. We take a unique and innovative approach to teaching that helps students connect with the subject matter they need to master

Accreditation

The overall continuity of WHTC rests on its ability to secure and maintain its accreditation at all material times and satisfying all regulatory compliance requirements. It is the responsibility of WHTC internal stakeholders to maintain its accreditation in all functions and activities. At the dawn of the 2021-25 Strategic Cycle, WHTC is remains being an Accredited Higher Education Institution in Namibia by the National Council for Higher Education, the Namibia Qualifications Authority, and The Health Professions Councils of Namibia among others.
