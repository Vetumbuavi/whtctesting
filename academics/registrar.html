Registrar & Admissions

Main Functions of the Department

WHTC's office of the Registar and Admissions main mission is to foster student academic progress by ensuring proper maintenance of academic records from first contact with students to graduation day.

The Registrar’s Office processes registration requests, schedules classes and maintains class lists, enforces the rules of entering or leaving the institute and keeps permanent records of student grades and marks. The Registrar's Office prepares statistical reports on educational activities for governmental and educational agencies (e.g., NQA, A/HPCNA, NCHE and other stakeholders).


Department Mandate

  • To advise on and foster a clear understanding of our systems, policies, processes and procedures.
  • To be able to be responsive, adaptable, and supportive of change is critical to our success.
  • To provide top quality service in our primary areas of responsibility.

    • Goals And Strategies

    The office of the Registrar strategic plan will help us focus our efforts in those areas where we can best support WHTC’s vision and mission. This plan will not only act as our guidepost, but will keep us focused on where, and how, we can become more efficient, effective, and productive. Even with our goals clearly outlined and defined, we must maintain an elevated level of flexibility and creativity to accomplish our objectives, all the while being agile enough to adapt to the changing needs of WHTC’s environment.

    Overall Functions of the Office of the Register

  • Registration and transcript services
  • Process WHTC’s Academic Calendar and printing of final exams
  • Schedule classes and maintain class lists and enforce rules for entering or leaving classes
  • Permanent Student Academic Records keeping and ensure students meet graduation requirements
  • Produce data about students classes and maintain the academic records of the institution
  • Room Scheduling and Registration of new and returning students
  • Printing of final Exams,Transcript services and Maintain the academic records of the institution

    About us

    Welwitchia Health training Centre (University) “Where everyone is an achiever”, is a private institute for higher learning education in Namibia with a special focus on Health, Social and Management Science. It has placed teaching and learning, research and development, innovation and technology solutions, industry and community service, continuous professional development, and training and capacity development at the center of its operation. We take a unique and innovative approach to teaching that helps students connect with the subject matter they need to master.
